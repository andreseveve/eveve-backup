Eveve Restaurant Booking Systems



Eveve provide the World's most dynamic live restaurant booking systems. The two key components of the system are the live table management system and the live booking pages. The former manages your availability for the latter, which is a conduit for new bookings.


We recognise that every restaurant is different, and we suit our systems to your restaurant - not the other way around. Some restaurants are looking to take advantage of structured online bookings, but do not need extensive table management. Other restaurants seek the opposite. Whatever your needs, it is likely we can accommodate them.


We provide three core systems, which in their various modified states, can cater for most restaurants' needs:



Online bookings only: The restaurant continues to use a paper diary, allocating a small number of tables for online. If these tables are otherwise used, their availability is modified with a very easy to use control console. Click here to find out more.


Live Table Management: The restaurant computerises its diary, and all bookings, whether online, telephone or walk in are recorded. Click here to find out more.


Client Relationship Management System: As the standard system, but records who is eating at the restaurant, together with useful information such as eating preferences, average spend, address, telephone number etc. Click here to find out more.



All three of these systems integrate effortlessly with EPOS tills, web sites, events, and room booking systems.
