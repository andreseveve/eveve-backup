Building on the online reservations, Eveve can provide fully computerised table management. Such a system manages all of the restaurants tables and all bookings for them: by telephone, through the internet, or made in person. There are key benefits to computerising your diary:

Internet bookings are free: the whole point of the internet is to save money. With our table management system you pay a modest monthly fee and all internet bookings are free, saving you money.

Multi-location booking: bookings can be updated from multiple consoles in real-time. In practical terms this means that a booking taken in the office automatically updates availability at the front desk instantly.

Fully automate internet bookings: all internet booking requests are cross referenced with live availability for all your tables. This means that a definitive confirmation can be sent out instantly without adding to your workload.

Improve booking structure: if required we can implement rules to guide less experienced members of staff in their allocation of reservations. These can be overwritten with a management password. For example, you may wish to set a maximum number of covers per half hour to reduce stress for the kitchen.

Accurate records: traditional paper diaries can become a mess by the end of the year and handwriting is often illegible. Moreover, little information can be extracted without prohibitive amounts of work. By computerising your diary you can make it fresh and crisp, while turning it into a valuable management tool, which can calculate your monthly covers, occupancy rates etc.
