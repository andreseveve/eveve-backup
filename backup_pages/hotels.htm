Eveve provide live hotel management systems, which combine inhouse room management with live structured reservations through the internet. The Eveve hotel management solutions offers:

Client Relationship Management: CRM combines all the functionality of live table management and structured online bookings with an integrated database of the hotel's customers.

Integration with EPOS till systems: which provides powerful management information, reduces duplication of work, and improves the customer experience.

Integration with Hotel Table Management: designed to improve information sharing between restaurant and hotel, and to increase conversion rate of guests dining in the restaurant.

Events management: allows you to take live bookings for weddings, cookery classes, wine tastings etc.
