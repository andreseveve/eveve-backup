More people than ever before are using the internet and booking restaurants online. If you are online you are tapping into this huge growing market. If you are not online, you are missing out.


Live online bookings through your website work 24/7 and regardless of how busy the restaurant is. How many times has the telephone rung out or hit engaged on a busy Friday night at 8pm, or not been answered early in the morning? Did the customer ring back or go elsewhere? With a live booking system, you can convert more of your website visitors by allowing every customer to book online at their convenience.





The case for an Eveve live restaurant booking system is more than just going online. Our software allows you to control the online booking process. The customer interacts with the table availability system to check for space. Moreover, their requests are made within the prism of your restaurant's rules, such as 10 covers per half hour; 40 seats total capacity; no groups over 10 etc. If there is space, they receive a confirmation, if not, an alternative time is offered. Eveve Live Restaurant Booking Systems offer distinct advantages:


Total Control: bookings are only allowed if they fit within your booking rules, which gives you total control. You have booking rules because structured bookings are more profitable, and reduce stress. Now your online bookings can be structured.


Reduce Workload: every email request needs a reply, which increases workload.


Instant Confirmation: a customer who receives instant confirmation is less inclined to book elsewhere, than one awaiting confirmation.
