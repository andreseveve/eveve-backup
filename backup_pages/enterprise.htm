Whatever your industry, if you have a resource that needs to be allocated in real time across multiple locations, Eveve can provide the reservation system to maximise your yield.

Eveve have over 10 years experience of building live, multi-user reservation systems for the most demanding of clients. Our Enterprise systems are geared towards medium to large companies and every one is custom built to their exact specification. Our portfolio is weighted towards companies in the advertising sector, because of the requirement that their portfolio is to be sold simultaneously by multiple salespeople.

While every Enterprise system is unique, they share the following characteristics:

Live and multi user: our system is multi client (connection) which means numerous users can interact with live resource availability in real time. If one salesperson in London sells advertising space, another in Liverpool is instantly prohibited from allocating that slot.

Maximise your inventory: sophisticated software algorithms maximise the yield from your resources. Eveve Enterprise systems have been noted to increase revenues for the respective products by 5%, an astounding cost-benefit ratio, given the modest price of the products.

Rapid delivery: Eveve utilise some of the industry's most highly skilled professionals to turn around custom built systems in a fraction of the time that larger, less skilled teams are capable of.

Cost effective: our rapid delivery times mean that we are highly competetive on price.
