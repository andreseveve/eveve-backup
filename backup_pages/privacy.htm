Eveve Privacy policy

Scope

This statement applies to Eveve.

Personal Information

We may collect information directly from you, when you register as a member or make a reservation on our website or a website of one of our affiliate partners or restaurant partners, subscribe to our newsletters, write to us, telephone us or email us. This will usually include your name and all of your contact details, for the purpose of making a restaurant reservation.

We may use cookies and other technologies to keep track of your interactions with our sites and services to offer a personalised experience.

Uses of Information

We use information about you for responding to and fulfilling your requests for our products and services. This includes sending you automated service messages related to your upcoming or recent restaurant reservations, such as confirmations, reminders, and/or invitations to provide user reviews & ratings. You cannot unsubscribe from all service messages.

We might send you newsletters or promotional offers from time to time. Our newsletters provide general news, information about the latest deals, and occasionally offer incentives for completing surveys or participating in competitions or promotions.

Where you have consented to receive marketing communications from Eveve NZ, you may from time to time receive communications relating to special offers and/or promotions.

We use information about you to help us to identify other services that might be beneficial to you, and to inform you about them and other items of interest.

We use information about you to help us with our internal accounting and administration, and to protect you and us from fraud.

We do NOT share any personal information about you with anyone, other than as outlined in this agreement, as may be required by law, or as needed by us to assist in managing our relationship with you.

The Internet is not a secure environment. If you send us information, including your email address, it is sent at your own risk.

Other information we collect

Statistical information

When you visit our site, we record general information about your visit for statistical purposes.

You may access the personal information we hold about you, and ask us to correct any errors.

Who We Share Your Information With

We do not disclose any information about customer bookings to anyone except in the following circumstances:

Restaurants

We provide key information including your name, telephone number and email address for each booking made to the relevant restaurant. Restaurants require this information for the booking and may use it if they need to contact you about the booking. We have placed limitations on these restaurants from using or disclosing your information without your permission. Despite this, we encourage you to review the privacy policies of any restaurant or other service provider whose services you utilise via our website.

Affiliate Partners

We have placed limitations on these affiliate partners from using or disclosing your information without your permission. Despite this, we encourage you to review the privacy policies of the relevant affiliate partner. You may also consent to receive marketing communications from the website at which you've made a reservation. Eveve NZ plays no role in the content, frequency, timing or un-subscription process of any affiliate partners restaurant's marketing communications. If you would like to unsubscribe from an affiliate partners marketing communications, please contact the appropriate affiliate partner directly.

Legal obligations

We may be required by law to disclose information you provide us with for the purposes of obtaining services. We may also disclose information against someone who poses a threat to our interests (such as customer fraud) or whose activities could cause harm to others.

How to control and access your information

You are able to control and have access to your information:

Newsletter subscription and unsubscription

If at any time you would like to either change your email address with us or unsubscribe from our newsletter or promotional correspondence, you can:

Go to the "Privacy" section on our website and follow the instructions to change your email address or unsubscribe; or Send an email to richard.small@eveve.com advising that you would like to unsubscribe or change your email address. Be sure to include details of your old and new email addresses for changes.

General

If at any time you wish to enquire about any of your personal information which we store, please contact us.

How we collect your information

To ensure the confidentiality of your personal information, we use leading security technology and procedures that are regularly reviewed and updated.
