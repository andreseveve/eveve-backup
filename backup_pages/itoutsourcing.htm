For many restaurant groups, IT is an ever growing issue for the business. Properly managed IT can be enormously powerful, but without focus it can be more of a headache.

An innovative modern solution is to outsource your restaurant's IT to a group which specialises in providing total restaurant solutions. The advantages of outsourcing your restaurant's IT are:

Knowledgable specialists: with Eveve you get top quality professionals whose primary focus is restaurants. This means we understand the specific issues and peculiarities of the environment and can hit the ground running.

Agreed uptime: Eveve have multiple technical staff, whose hours are pooled. This means we can provide technical support beyond the normal hours, if required.

Fast repairs: our highly competent engineers can diagnose and repair problems quickly.

Fewer problems: a properly structured IT environment means one problems are less likely, and can be diagnosed more quickly. Eveve can help organise your setup so you will have fewer problems in the future.

Cost effective: the cost of outsourcing your IT support is a fraction of the cost of having an in-house technical team, for the same, if not more uptime.
